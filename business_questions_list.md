# Hospital Management — Business Questions

26 business questions grouped by difficulty, used to analyze the Hospital
Management dataset (Snowflake SQL).

---

## SECTION A: Basic (SELECT, WHERE, ORDER BY)

1. List all patients from Tamil Nadu.
2. Show all Completed appointments.
3. Find all appointments with a billing amount above ₹10,000 (high-value cases).
4. How many patients are Male vs Female?
5. List all doctors and their department, sorted by experience.

## SECTION B: Joins & Aggregation

6. What is the total revenue generated by each department? (department revenue)
7. How many appointments has each doctor handled? (doctor workload)
8. What is the average treatment cost by appointment type?
9. How many times has each patient visited the hospital? (patient visit frequency)
10. Monthly appointment volume and revenue for the year.
11. Full appointment detail: patient, doctor, department, date, cost.
12. What is the cancellation / no-show rate by department?

## SECTION C: Subqueries & HAVING

13. Departments generating more than ₹50,00,000 in total revenue.
14. Doctors whose average treatment cost is above the hospital-wide average.
15. Patients who have never had a completed appointment (only cancelled/no-show).
16. Top 5 patients by total billing amount (highest hospital spend).
17. Doctors who handled more appointments than the average doctor.

## SECTION D: CASE Statements & Date Functions

18. Bucket completed appointments into cost tiers.
19. Patient age (as of today) and an age group, for demographic reporting.
20. How many new patients registered each year?
21. Which weekday sees the highest patient footfall?

## SECTION E: Window Functions & CTEs (Advanced)

22. Rank doctors within their department by number of appointments handled.
23. Running total of revenue per department over the year.
24. Using a CTE: the top-earning doctor in each department.
25. Month-over-month growth (%) in hospital revenue.
26. Patient visit leaderboard: dense rank all patients by number of visits.
